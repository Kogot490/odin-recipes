Pineapple Sangria

This is a tasty Pineapple Sangria recipe that anyone can make!

Serves 8 with a yield of one gallon. Prep time 25 min.

Ingredients

  • 2 (750 milliliter) bottles Sauvignon Blanc
  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1 cup triple sec
  • 1/2 cup brandy
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1/2 cup chopped orange
  • 1 lemon, seeded and chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped lime
  • 2 cups lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage (such as 7 up)

Steps

  1. Mix Sauvignon Blanc, pinmeapple juice, triple sec, and brandy together in a large pitcher. Add chopped pineapple, orange, lemon, and lime. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Stir in lemon-lime soda before serving.
